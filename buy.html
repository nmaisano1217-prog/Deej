Questions

What exactly do I get when I subscribe?

Within a few hours of subscribing you'll get an email with your personal license key and a link to the app. Enter your email and the key once per device and you're in.

Does it need internet on the job site?

No. Once the app is open on your device it runs fully offline — 3D previews, pricing, photos and printing all work with zero signal.

Can I use it on my phone AND my tablet?

Yes — your key works on all your own devices. Enter the same email and key on each one.

Are the prices real quotes?

They're planning estimates built from your base rate, shown as a range and clearly labeled "not a quote." You stay in control of final pricing.

How do I cancel?

Cancel your subscription anytime — you keep access through the period you've paid for, and you just won't receive the next key.

Do you offer refunds?

If it's not for you, email us within 14 days of your first payment and we'll refund it, no questions asked.
